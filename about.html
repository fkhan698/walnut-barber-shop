About Us

The barbers here at the Walnut Street Barber Shop have proudly been providing the men of the Springfield & surrounding area with, professional & excellent service since 1943. Experience matters to you! All of our barbers have years of experience - they understand the cuts you want and are sure to provide you with the haircut you request. An experienced barber will make the difference between the cut you want and the cut you get. Excellence matters to you!

The Springfield community has formally recognized our excellence over the years. · We have been voted “best barber shop” by the readers of the Springfield News-Leader. · We have been featured on the cover of 417 Magazine and have been voted Best of 417 numerous times. · We have been voted “best barber shop” by the readers of the Missouri State University Standard year-after-year. Price matters to you! It is important to us that we keep our rates fair and reasonable.
